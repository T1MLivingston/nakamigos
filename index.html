Lore

The story behind Nakamigos, HiFo Labs, and the community.

Nakamigos are 20,000 unique 24x24 pixel characters on Ethereum, "friends of Nakamoto." Created by the anonymous HiFo Labs, the collection appeared on October 31, 2022, the 14th anniversary of the Bitcoin whitepaper.

HiFo Labs went on to release Nakamigos-CLOAKS (20,000 characters with worldwide gaming rights), Crypto Trading Cards (1880-1979), Hal Froggy Bobbleheads, and Fukuhedrons on Bitcoin Ordinals. Holders receive full commercial rights to their NFTs.

Nakamigo.ai emerged as a community-driven AI project, not affiliated with HiFo Labs, but rooted in the same culture. It brings together the MigoStrategy token, community tools, and a growing ecosystem of games and experiments built by Nakamigos holders.

The lore is still being written. Every game shipped, every tool built, every community call, it all adds to the story.
